Here is a list of three fun activities you can try in Cape Town, South Africa

  • Hiking Table Mountain
  • Cable car ride up Table Mountain
  • Take a sunset boat cruise from V&A waterfront

Hiking Table Mountain

Did you know that table mountain is 1085m high? The mountain is hiked via several trails, such as Platteklip Gorge, Skeleton George or india Vester. The hike takes about 1.5 to 3 hours and hikers should bring proper shoes, bottle of water and comfortable clothes.

Cable car ride up Table Mountain

A cable car takes visitors from lower station to the upper section of the plateau. The journey takes 4-5 minutes and you should bring a jacket, sunglasses and hat.

Take a sunset boat cruise from V&A waterfront

Sunset champagne cruises takes about 1.5 hours and includes a local sparkling wine. Evenings on the water get chilly so you should ring a jacket.
